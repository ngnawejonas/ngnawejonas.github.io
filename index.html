About Me

"Wir mussen wissen. Wir werden wissen."(We must know. We will know.) David Hilbert

I am a PhD student at Mila-Quebec AI Institute and Université Laval (IID/LSVN lab). I work in the area of Adversarial Machine Learning under the supervision of Prof. Christian Gagné(Mila & Université Laval) and co-supervised by Prof. Frédéric Precioso(INRIA & Université Côte d’Azur). I was previously a Google AI resident at the Accra Lab where I was mentored by Yann Dauphin.
